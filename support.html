Sonar Support and FAQs

Having a problem with Sonar? Here are a few commonly asked questions:


1. When is the app launching?

The app will launch June 1st, 2024!


2. How much do customization options cost?

The costs of each type of customization are shown behind the customization options. So in this screenshot, we can see the player cosmetics cost 100 coins and color themes cost 500 coins.


3. How do I turn off sound? What about vibration?

You can use the settings gear in the bottom left to adjust your sound/vibration preferences.


4. Are you collecting any of my data? Do you have a privacy policy?

We have a privacy policy at https://sonar.sabercpp.com/privacy-policy


5. How can I get more help? What if I have a question not listed here?

Feel free to reach out to sonar@sabercpp.com with any questions or issues.
